Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of the most common questions we receive about custom 3D printing.

Why can't you provide an exact price?

Every model differs in size, material, print time, infill, supports, and finishing. We calculate each quotation individually to ensure fair and accurate pricing.

Can I provide my own STL file?

Absolutely. We accept most printable STL, 3MF, and OBJ files. If necessary, we'll review the model and suggest any improvements before printing.

What if I only have an idea?

No problem. We also offer CAD modeling and printable file preparation services to help turn your concept into a printable design.

Do you ship nationwide?

Yes. Orders can be picked up locally or shipped throughout Indonesia using your preferred courier service.
